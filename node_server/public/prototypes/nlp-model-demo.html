How it Works

This demo shows how you can use natural language to add 3D models to your CirclesXR prototype scenes. Simply describe what you want to add, and the AI will:

  1. Parse your natural language description
  2. Identify the models you mentioned
  3. Find the corresponding .glb files from our asset library
  4. Add them to your scene with appropriate positioning
